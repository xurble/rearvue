Instagram Setup

To connect to Instagram using the Graph API, you'll need:

  1. Instagram Business or Creator Account ID - This is the numeric ID of your Instagram account
  2. Facebook Page Access Token - A long-lived access token for your Facebook page

How to get these:

  1. Go to Facebook Developers
  2. Create or select your app
  3. Add Instagram Basic Display product to your app
  4. Connect your Instagram Business/Creator account
  5. Get your Instagram Account ID from the Instagram Basic Display settings
  6. Generate a Page Access Token for your Facebook page
